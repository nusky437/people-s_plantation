Who We Are

Company Overview

People’s Development Plantation is a Sri Lanka–based agricultural enterprise focused on building a sustainable and diversified plantation portfolio. The company invests in key sectors including paddy cultivation, aloe vera farming, seafood production, and vegetable farms, ensuring a balanced approach to both land and aquatic agriculture

With a strong commitment to quality, innovation, and environmental responsibility, People’s Development Plantation integrates modern farming techniques with traditional expertise to maximize productivity and maintainecological balance. The company’s operations are designed to support local communities, enhance food security, and contribute to the country’s agricultural growth
